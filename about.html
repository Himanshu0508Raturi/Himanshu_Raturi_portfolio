About Me

Get to know me!

Hello! I'm Himanshu Raturi, a Computer Science student passionate about Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Generative AI. I enjoy building intelligent systems that combine strong ML foundations with real-world applications, ranging from Computer Vision models to Large Language Model (LLM) powered solutions.

My recent work focuses on Generative AI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), where I have built systems for information retrieval, semantic search, and context-aware question answering. I have also worked on transfer learning for computer vision tasks and parameter-efficient fine-tuning of LLMs using techniques such as QLoRA. Through these projects, I have gained hands-on experience with PyTorch, TensorFlow, Hugging Face Transformers, vector databases, and modern ML workflows.

I am particularly interested in learning the mathematical and algorithmic foundations behind machine learning and applying them to build scalable, impactful AI systems. Amazon ML Summer School presents an exciting opportunity for me to deepen my understanding of ML concepts and learn from researchers working on cutting-edge machine learning problems.
